Gaurav Bharaj

 

I am Director of Research at AI Foundation, where I lead Deep Science research for generative media creation. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science from Harvard University under the supervision of Prof. Hanspeter Pfister and Masters from University of Pennsylvania. Previously, I worked at Max-Planck Institute of Informatics, Disney, Adobe, Intel and Technicolor Research.

My research interests lie at the intersection of computer vision, graphics and audio; where I apply deep learning, numerical optimization, and generative modeling to understand aspects of video-based visual - geometry, color, motion and audio content, especially for digital humans.
